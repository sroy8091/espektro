About

  • 2006

    ESPEKTRO is the Annual Techno-Management and Cultural festival of Kalyani Government Engineering College, held in the month of March. It started with the year of 2006, and since then it has continued to soar to greater heights, spreading out its wings across an expansive arena.

  • 2015

    A platform that showcased youthful energy, intellectuality and creativity, the festival spanned across three days and nights of high octane action, a perfect blend of exuberance, talent and excellence.

  • 2016

    Bringing our world into a different dimension, Espektro'16 was when we brought the skies of the Arab world to our home-ground. The technical, cultural and managerial spirits mingled to bring forth fizz, frolic and festivity while the Pro-Nites casted a spell on one, an all.

  • 2017

    9th, 10th and 11th of March - The three days and nights in a boisterous juncture, will be including technical events in the mornings and cultural events in the evenings. And as an added treat, this year, will be an escapade, into 'A world of novels'.
