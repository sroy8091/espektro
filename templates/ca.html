ROLE OF A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

Campus Ambassadors are liaisons between Espektro and their university. The Campus Ambassador program has been undertaken by Espektro, KGEC to incorporate participation from students spread across the country, specially the outskirts of Kolkata. As a Campus Ambassador you shall be co-ordinating with Espektro to ensure maximum participation from your college in Espektro'17 through :

  • Online publicity of Espektro via social media in your college.
  • Sharing each and every event post shared in the Facebook page of Espektro, KGEC and Techtix, KGEC in respective Whatsapp and Facebook groups.
  • Promoting Espektro, KGEC in your campus through posters, word of mouth etc.

WHY BE A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR?

  • Unique opportunity to develop your communication and leadership skills
  • Certificate from Espektro, KGEC.
  • Exciting Gift Hampers.
  • Prizes worth 5000 rupees on being the best among all other Campus Ambassadors.
  • Leverages during participation.
