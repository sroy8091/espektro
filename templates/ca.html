Campus Ambassador Programme

ROLE OF A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

The Campus Ambassador program has been undertaken by Espektro KGEC to incorporate participation from students spread across the state, specially the outskirts of Kolkata

As a Campus Ambassador you shall be co-ordinating with Espektro to ensure maximum participation from your college through

  • Online publicity of Espektro by sharing each and every event post shared in the pages of Espektro KGEC and Tectix KGEC in respective facebook pages and groups
  • Promoting ESPEKTRO, KGEC in your campus
  • Promoting our events through the various communication channels (fb page, online notice board, word of mouth etc.) in your campus
  • Ensuring good participation number from your college in our events
  • Sharing each and every event, post shared in the page Espektro, KGEC and TECHTIX, KGEC in respective Whatsapp groups and FB groups.

Incentives for CAs:

  • Certificate from Espektro,KGEC.
  • Exciting Goodies and leverages during participation.
  • Prizes worth 5000 rupees on being the best among all CAs.
